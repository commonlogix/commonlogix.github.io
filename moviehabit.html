Built Different
MovieHabit is not a streaming guide — it's a cinephile universe that lives on your device and respects your obsession.

No account. No ads. No cloud. No social pressure. Just a private sanctuary for people who take film seriously — powered by on-device AI, built by one developer who believes cinema deserves better software.

Built for the obsessed.

Most movie apps are built for casual scrollers. MovieHabit was built for people who care — who have opinions about cinematography, who can name the DP on a 1970s Italian horror film, who argue about the correct order to watch a director's filmography.

The app is fully offline and privacy-first by design. No account is required. No personal data is sold. No third-party tracking. Your watch history, your ratings, your taste profile — all of it stays on your device.

Why MovieHabit Exists

MovieHabit was built because no app existed that treated cinephiles like adults. Every major movie app is either a streaming aggregator, a social network, or a glorified spreadsheet. None of them felt like they were built by someone who actually loves film.

This app started from a simple question: what would the perfect tool look like for someone who is genuinely obsessed with cinema? The answer is a universe — rating, tracking, exploring connections, playing games, chatting with an AI that knows its stuff, and building a personal archive that reflects who you are as a viewer.

It runs on Apple Foundation Models, stores everything locally, and will never require an account or sell your data. Because a great cinephile app should respect the person using it.
