Contact

For support, questions, or feedback, email us at: info@commonlogix.com

To help us respond faster, please include:

  • App name (for example, FaithHabit)
  • Your device model and iOS version
  • What you were trying to do when the issue happened
  • Any error message you saw (if applicable)

Subscriptions & Billing

If you purchased a subscription through the Apple App Store, billing and subscription management are handled by Apple. You can manage or cancel your subscription from your device: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

If you’re having trouble accessing Pro features after purchasing, email us and include the email address used for your Apple ID (if you’re comfortable sharing it) and the steps you’ve tried.

CommonLogix apps are built with a privacy-first mindset. Many features are designed to work offline and keep your content on your device. Review our Privacy Policy for details.
