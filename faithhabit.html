What it does

  • Daily actions that don’t guilt-trip you Log Mass, prayer, reading, saint, and journaling with a calm, gentle tone.
  • A full Bible built into the app Fast navigation, bookmark favorite passages, and pick up where you left off.
  • Saint of the Day + daily reflection Short, grounded inspiration that connects the Gospel to modern life.
  • Progress that feels like grace Encouragement, not grinding. Streaks are never “broken.”
