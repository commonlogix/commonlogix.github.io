What FaithHabit helps you do

  • Log Mass, prayer, Gospel reading, saints, and journaling in seconds
  • Build a simple daily rhythm without guilt or pressure

Built for privacy

FaithHabit is designed to feel calm and personal. No tracking. No ads. No social feeds. The goal is a quiet tool that helps you show up.

Offline-first

Faith shouldn’t require Wi-Fi. FaithHabit is built to work reliably without internet after install, so your daily habit stays steady wherever you are.
