Privacy Policy

Introduction

CommonLogix LLC ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle information when you use our mobile applications, websites, and services.

Information We Collect

We believe in collecting only what's necessary. Here's what we do and don't collect:

  • Information You Provide: When you use our apps, you may voluntarily provide information such as preferences, settings, or content you create within the app.
  • Usage Information: We may collect basic usage data to understand how our apps perform and improve user experience. This is done anonymously whenever possible.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app functionality and crash reporting.
  • We Do NOT Collect: Personal data, location data, health data, or any other sensitive information unless explicitly required by the app's core functionality and with your consent.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information only for these purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining our services
  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Diagnosing and fixing technical issues
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Protecting against fraud and misuse

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use third-party services for analytics, crash reporting, or app functionality. These services have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices. Check their policies directly.

App Store Privacy

When you download our apps from the Apple App Store or other platforms, those platforms have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your data, including the right to access, correct, or delete information. Contact us at info@commonlogix.com to exercise these rights.
