Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

Introduction

CommonLogix LLC ("CommonLogix," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect (if any), how we use it, and the choices you have when you use our websites and mobile applications.

Offline-First Apps (On-Device Data)

Many CommonLogix apps are designed to work offline. In those apps, your content (for example, notes, journal entries, preferences, and settings) is stored locally on your device. We do not receive that on-device content unless you explicitly choose to contact us and include it in your message.

Information We Collect

We collect only what is needed to operate our services and provide support.

  • Information you provide to us: If you email us for support, we receive the information you include (such as your email address and the contents of your message).
  • Website information: Our website host may automatically log standard technical information (such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited) to keep the site secure and functioning.
  • Diagnostics and crash information (if enabled): Some apps may use platform-provided diagnostics or crash reporting to help us identify and fix bugs. These reports are intended to contain technical data about app performance and errors, not the private content you create in the app.

What We Do Not Collect

Unless a specific app feature clearly requires it (and you choose to use that feature), we do not collect:

  • Precise location data
  • Contact lists or address book data
  • Photos, camera data, or microphone recordings
  • Private app content stored locally on your device (such as personal notes or journals)
  • Sensitive personal data such as health data

How We Use Information

We use information for the following limited purposes:

  • To respond to support requests and customer service inquiries
  • To maintain and improve app reliability, performance, and security
  • To comply with applicable laws and enforce our terms
  • To protect against fraud, abuse, and misuse

Third-Party Services

Some apps may rely on third-party services for distribution, payments, and platform functionality:

  • App Store / Payments: If you purchase a subscription or in-app purchase, the transaction is handled by the platform provider (for example, Apple). We do not receive your full payment details.
  • Hosting: Our website may be hosted by a third-party provider that processes standard access logs to operate and secure the website.
  • Diagnostics (optional): If an app uses crash reporting, those providers process technical crash data on our behalf.

Third-party providers have their own privacy policies and practices. We encourage you to review them.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above. Support emails and related records may be retained to help resolve issues and improve support quality. On-device app content remains on your device unless you choose to share it with us.

Your Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have certain rights regarding your information, including the right to access, correct, or delete information we hold about you.

  • On-device data: You can delete app content stored on your device by using in-app delete options (if available) or uninstalling the app.
  • Support communications: You may request deletion of support emails you have sent us by contacting us.

To make a request, contact us at info@commonlogix.com.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

Security

We take reasonable measures designed to protect information we process. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of our services after an update means you accept the updated policy.
